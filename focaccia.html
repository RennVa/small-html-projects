Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your tastebyds with this festive focaccia appetizer with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extravirgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though isnot necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. 1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though sometimes I knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
